Our Story

Born from a passion for honest food and warm hospitality, Lubanzi's Eatery has become a destination for those who believe that great meals are made with intention — and shared with joy.

Where It All Began

Lubanzi's Eatery opened with a singular vision: to create a space where people could gather, slow down, and experience food that tells a story. Every dish on our menu reflects that founding purpose.

Nestled in Umhlanga, Durban, we've grown from a neighbourhood eatery into a full dining destination — offering breakfast, lunch, dinner and everything in between. Our kitchen sources local ingredients wherever possible and our chefs treat every plate like a canvas.

Whether you're joining us for a quiet morning espresso or an indulgent evening dinner, you'll always leave with a full stomach and a warm heart.

The Dining Experience

From the candlelit warmth of our evening service to the bright, buzzing energy of our morning rush — Lubanzi's is designed to feel like home.

Our menu spans a full day: hearty breakfasts, satisfying lunch bowls, elevated dinner plates, and an extensive drinks menu crafted by our in-house baristas. Every visit is a new experience.

We also cater for private dining, corporate events and celebrations — contact us to plan your perfect occasion at Lubanzi's.
