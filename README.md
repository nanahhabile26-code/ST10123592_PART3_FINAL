1.1  Brief History
Lubanzi's Eatery is a premier dining destination nestled in Umhlanga, Durban. Founded with a singular passion for honest, intentional food and warm hospitality, the eatery has grown from a neighbourhood favourite into a full-service restaurant offering breakfast, lunch, dinner, and everything in between.
From the bright, buzzing energy of morning service to the candlelit warmth of evening dining, Lubanzi's is designed to feel like home. The kitchen sources local ingredients wherever possible, and every plate is treated as a canvas — reflecting both culinary craftsmanship and genuine care for the guest experience.
Lubanzi's also provides private dining, corporate event catering, and celebration services, solidifying its position as one of Durban's most beloved and versatile dining destinations.
1.2  Mission Statement
"To create a space where people gather, slow down, and experience food that tells a story — crafted with intention and shared with joy."
1.3  Vision Statement
"To be Durban's most celebrated full-service eatery, recognised for culinary excellence, community warmth, and a dining experience that keeps guests coming back."
1.4  Core Values
•	Culinary Excellence — Quality in every ingredient, every preparation, every plate.
•	Local & Fresh — Partnering with local KZN suppliers for seasonal, farm-to-table ingredients.
•	Community — More than an eatery; a gathering place for Durban's food lovers, neighbours, and friends.
•	Made with Heart — Care in the kitchen and warmth at the front of house on every visit.
1.5  Target Audience
•	Durban residents and tourists seeking quality coastal dining experiences.
•	Business professionals looking for corporate lunch, dinner, and event venues.
•	Families, couples, and groups celebrating milestones and special occasions.
•	Food enthusiasts and brunch culture followers in the Umhlanga area.
•	Event planners seeking private dining and catering services.

SECTION 3
Current Digital Presence Analysis

Lubanzi's Eatery currently operates a multi-page HTML website comprising a Home page, About page, Services (Menu) page, and Contact page. The following analysis has been conducted to inform the redesign and enhancement strategy.
3.1  Strengths of the Existing Website
•	Well-structured four-page layout covering core business information.
•	Clear navigation with submenu for menu categories (Breakfast & Lunch, Dinner, Beverages).
•	Contact page includes physical address, phone, email, operating hours, and a working enquiry form.
•	About page communicates brand story, values, and dining experience effectively.
•	Footer includes newsletter subscription, social media links, and quick navigation.
•	Slideshow hero on homepage creates an engaging first impression.
3.2  Weaknesses and Areas for Improvement
•	No integrated online booking or reservation engine — all bookings handled manually.
•	No actual menu items, prices, or dish descriptions on the services page — content is a placeholder.
•	Images rely on local file paths — no CDN or image optimisation strategy in place.
•	Missing SEO strategy: no meta descriptions, structured data, or keyword optimisation.
•	No live chat, WhatsApp integration, or instant guest support mechanism.
•	No Google Maps embed on the Contact page despite address being listed.
•	Social media icons present but not linked to active profiles.
•	No blog or content marketing section to drive organic search traffic.
•	No TripAdvisor, Google Reviews, or testimonials widget to build trust.

SECTION 5
Design and User Experience

5.1  Design Aesthetic and Branding
The website design will reflect Lubanzi's Eatery's identity: warm, authentic dining culture with a modern, polished sensibility. The visual language will communicate quality, community, and comfort — appealing to Durban's discerning food lovers.

Design Style	Modern Warmth with African Coastal Influences
Inspiration	Warm ivory tones, copper accents, candlelit textures, lush greenery
Aesthetic	Clean editorial layout; premium but approachable and welcoming
5.2  Colour Scheme

Warm Gold  #C9973A	Ivory White  #FBF7EE	Rich Bark  #1A1008	Copper  #8B4513
Primary Accent	Background	Headings & Text	Supporting Accent
5.3  Typography

Primary Heading Font	Cormorant Garamond — Elegant serif for restaurant name, page titles, and hero text
Secondary Heading Font	Montserrat (SemiBold) — Clean sans-serif for section headings
Body Copy Font	Open Sans (Regular/Light) — Highly readable for menu descriptions and content
Accent / CTA Font	Montserrat (Bold, uppercase) — Used for buttons and labels
Font Hierarchy	H1: 48–64px | H2: 32–36px | H3: 22–26px | Body: 16px | Captions: 13px
5.4  Layout and Design Approach
•	Full-screen hero slideshow on homepage with an overlay reservation/enquiry CTA button.
•	Menu cards displayed in a responsive grid with hover effects revealing dish highlights and pricing.
•	Sticky navigation bar that transitions from transparent to solid on scroll.
•	Generous whitespace to create a premium, uncluttered feel throughout.
•	Asymmetric editorial layouts in the About and private dining sections for visual interest.
•	Full-bleed image sections alternating with text panels to create visual rhythm.
5.5  User Experience Considerations
•	Navigation — Maximum three-click depth to any page; clear primary navigation with dropdown menus.
•	Accessibility — WCAG 2.1 AA compliant; sufficient colour contrast, alt text for all images, keyboard navigation.
•	Booking Flow — Streamlined 2-step process: Enter Details → Submit Enquiry / Confirm Reservation.
•	Page Load Speed — Target under 2.5 seconds; lazy image loading and CDN implementation.
•	Trust Signals — Prominently displayed guest reviews, Google star ratings, and security indicators.
5.6  Key Page Wireframe Descriptions

Page	Layout Description
Homepage	Sticky navbar | Hero slideshow with booking CTA overlay | USP strip (3 icons) | Menu preview grid | Testimonials carousel | Instagram feed | Footer
Menu / Services	Navbar | Section anchors (Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner, Beverages) | Dish card grid (image, name, description, price) | Footer
About	Navbar | Story split-section (text + image, alternating) | Values grid (4 cards) | CTA strip | Footer
Contact	Navbar | Contact info cards (Phone, Email, Address, Hours) | Embedded Google Map | Contact/Reservation form | WhatsApp CTA | Footer
 
SECTION 6
Technical Requirements

6.1  Hosting and Domain

Domain Name	www.lubanziseatery.co.za (or .com)
Domain Registrar	Domains.co.za or Afrihost (South African registrar recommended)
Hosting Type	Cloud VPS Hosting — for performance and scalability
Hosting Provider	Hetzner South Africa (local CDN for low latency)
SSL Certificate	Let's Encrypt (Free) or Comodo Positive SSL — mandatory for booking system
Email Hosting	Professional email (e.g., reservations@lubanziseatery.co.za) via Zoho Mail or G Suite
CDN	Cloudflare — for performance, security, and South African edge servers
Backup	Daily automated backups with 30-day retention
6.2  Technology Stack

Frontend	HTML5, CSS3 (CSS Variables & Grid/Flexbox), JavaScript (ES6+)
CSS Framework	Tailwind CSS — rapid, consistent utility-based styling
Database	MySQL (via WordPress) with Redis caching
Version Control	Git (GitHub) — for codebase management and collaboration
